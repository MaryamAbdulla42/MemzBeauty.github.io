HAND MASKS

Ingredients:

  1. 6tbsp sugar
  2. 1 tbsp lemon juice
  3. 3 tbsp cocunut/olive oil

Blend all ingredients and apply until dry and wash off with warm water

This mask moisturizes the hand

Ingredients:

  1. 2 tbsp honey
